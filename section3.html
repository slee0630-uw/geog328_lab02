Care and Health

Because of their thick coat, Pomeranians need regular brushing, ideally 2–3 times a week, to prevent matting and tangles. They should also have routine dental care, as small breeds are prone to tooth issues. Regular vet check-ups help monitor common conditions such as:

  • Luxating patella (loose kneecap)
  • Tracheal collapse
  • Dental disease

A balanced diet and daily playtime are essential for maintaining their health and happiness.
